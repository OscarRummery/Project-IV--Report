\section{Introduction}
This document illustrates the use of BibTeX\@.  You may want to refer to
\cite{boyd2001chebyshev} or \cite{hussaini1983spectral} or \cite{haidvogel1979accurate}.  Or you may want to cite a
specific page in a reference, like this: see \citet[p.~199]{boyd2001chebyshev}.  Or
perhaps you want to cite more than one paper by Maskin: \cite{hussaini1983spectral, haidvogel1979accurate}.
Or you want to make a parenthetical reference to one or more articles, in which case the \verb+\citealt+ command omits the parentheses around the year (\citealt{haidvogel1979accurate}).
